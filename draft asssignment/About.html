Our Story

Salsavor was born from a passion for Latin dance and community. In 2010, Andres O. Mendez planted the seed, as he liked to say, to create a space where dancers could grow together. He started teaching from his home before expanding to various venues. By 2014, Salsavor was officially established as a startup.

The pandemic was a challenging time, but today, our academy has been revitalized and now welcomes over **290 students and members**. We invite you to become part of our family!
