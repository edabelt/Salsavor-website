Genres & Styles

Salsa

Born from Cuban roots, Salsa is a vibrant and dynamic dance that blends Afro-Caribbean rhythms with energetic partner work. Popular worldwide, it includes styles like Cuban, L.A., and New York Salsa. Its fast footwork and playful turns make it an exciting social dance experience.

Bachata

Originating from the Dominican Republic, Bachata is a sensual dance known for its intimate connection and smooth hip movements. It has evolved into styles like Dominican, Sensual, and Modern Bachata. The rhythm is driven by guitar melodies, making it deeply expressive and romantic.

Kizomba

Hailing from Angola, Kizomba is a soulful and elegant dance emphasizing musicality and fluid movements. It is danced in close connection, creating a smooth and intimate experience. The music blends African rhythms with a slow and captivating beat.
